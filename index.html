About my work at BeeHealthy

At BeeHealthy, I divide my time between a team of designers and a team of developers. I work closely with different product development disciplines. I help evolve processes around accessibility, design systems and cross-team collaboration.

My key product area is BeeHealthy's Digital Clinic offering. I help develop services and tools for medical professionals and patients alike. I aim to develop professional tools that are powerful, yet straightforward and accessible.

Nordea Design

About my work at Nordea

50+ designers and 100+ developers work on bringing Nordea's digital products to life.

At Nordea, I was part of the Design Language team. The Design Language team's goal was to provide tools and resources to the design community, enhance common ways of working, and promote accessible design and development practices.

My work as Senior UX Designer included:

  • Research, validation, and creation of design documentation about UX principles, accessibility and visual design
  • Nordea Design System maintenance and evolution
  • Development of cross-disciplinary design processes
  • Facilitation of workshops and knowledge-sharing activities to promote collaboration
  • Communication of the team's services to stakeholders
  • Creation of vector and 3D visual assets
