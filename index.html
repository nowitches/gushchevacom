About my work at BeeHealthy

As a designer at BeeHealthy, I work on a wide variety of tasks. My main task is the product design for BeeHealthy's Digital Clinic. Private and public health care providers use BeeHealthy's platform. Digital Clinic allows fast access to on-demand digital health care services. Patients can discuss their health-related concerns with health care specialists.
