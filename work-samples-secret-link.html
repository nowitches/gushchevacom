  • BeeHealthy

    At BeeHealthy, my main area of work is the Digital Clinic for patients and medical professionals.

    The Digital Clinic product offering includes:

    • Patient-oriented cross-platform applications for receiving digital medical services
    • Medical professional-oriented tools for managing Digital Clinic discussions and providing medical services
    • Administrator-oriented tools for managing digital services and relationships

    The main challenges that I tackle at BeeHealthy include:

    • Complex problems for different customer groups: I solve challenges that concern a wide variety of customer groups, from patients to service providers
    • Design and research processes: I promote shared design practices and advocate for user research, as well as more direct access to BeeHealthy clients for informed design decisions
    • Accessibility: in addition to general work around promoting accessibility, I work with with ensuring compliance with formal legiaslation as BeeHealthy's clients operate in the public sector

  • Nordea Bank

    My main project within Nordea was the Nordea Design System.

    The main challenges that I tackled included:

    • Consistency of experiences: bringing the experience of Nordea Digital Services to harmony through design system and UX work
    • Communication silos: collaborating with teams through workshops, establishing clear channels and documented principles
    • Lack of design documentation: researching, developing and documenting common UX pattern on nordea.design
    • Accessibility knowledge gaps: hosting workshops and sparring sessions with designers and developers
