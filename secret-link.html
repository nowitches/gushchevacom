BeeHealthy

2022-

As a designer at BeeHealthy, I work on a wide variety of tasks. My main task is the product design for BeeHealthy's Digital Clinic. Private and public health care providers such as Mediclinic and Länsi-Uudenmaan hyvinvointialue use BeeHealthy's platform.

Digital Clinic allows fast access to on-demand digital health care services. Patients can discuss their health-related concerns with health care specialists.

Digihyvinvointi (in Finnish)

My work includes:

  • Solving cross-platform design problems for patients, specialists and clinic admins
  • Researching and validating solutions
  • Delivering high-fidelity specifications and closely collaborating with devs and product managers
  • Content design and localization process management
  • Encouraging cross-team collaboration through shared processes

Nordea

2019-2022

I worked on Nordea's Design System as part of the Design Language team. I guided others in successfully implementing the Design System in their work. I advocated for accessibility and inclusive design. I also developed and documented UX and visual design patterns.

nordea.design Nordean mobiilipankki voitti parhaan digitaalisen palvelun Grand Prix -palkinnon (in Finnish)

The challenges I tackled included:

  • Consistency of experiences: bringing the experience of Nordea Digital Services to harmony through design system and UX work
  • Communication silos: collaborating with teams through workshops, establishing clear channels and documented principles
  • Lack of design documentation: researching, developing and documenting common UX pattern on nordea.design
  • Accessibility knowledge gaps: hosting workshops and sparring sessions with designers and developers
